# Refund Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-01. This is the document our LLM reads when asked
to evaluate a refund request. It deliberately reflects the kind of prose
you'd find on a real policy page — readable, hedged in places, with
genuine ambiguity at the edges. Our rule artifact encodes the same
logic deterministically.

## 1. Standard returns for change of mind

We accept returns on physical items within **30 days of purchase** if the
item is in original condition. Refunds for change-of-mind returns after
30 days are not accepted.

For **digital goods** (downloads, software licenses, subscription content),
change-of-mind returns are not accepted once the item has been used or
consumed.

## 2. Defective items

If a product is defective, we accept refund requests for up to **one year
from the date of purchase**. Defects include manufacturing issues, missing
components, damage on arrival, and non-functioning downloads. After the
1-year window, defect claims are handled as warranty requests rather than
refunds.

## 3. Wrong item received

If we shipped the wrong product, we always issue a refund or replacement.
This applies regardless of time elapsed, item condition, or usage — the
error is ours.

## 4. Duplicate or accidental charges

Customers who report a duplicate charge are issued an automatic refund
without further review. The original transaction is retained; only the
duplicate is reversed.

## 5. Billing errors and disputes

For billing errors (wrong amount charged, charge for a cancelled order,
pricing mismatches), requests are routed to our finance team for manual
review. These are not auto-approved or auto-denied by policy.

## 6. Abuse prevention

Accounts with more than **three refunds in the past 90 days** are routed
to a manual review queue before any new refund is processed. This is
not a denial — it's a check for unusual patterns.

## 7. Large orders

Orders over **$500** from non-enterprise customers require manager
approval before refund. Enterprise accounts have contract-specific refund
terms that supersede the standard policy; those are handled outside of
this document.

---

*This policy may be updated without notice. In case of ambiguity, a
support agent's judgment applies. We reserve the right to deny refunds
for clear abuse or fraud.*
